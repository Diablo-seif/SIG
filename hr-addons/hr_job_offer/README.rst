Job Offer
=========

.. class:: text-left

Features
--------

- Ability to print pdf job offer
- Ability to send pdf job offer to the applicant

.. class:: text-left

How to Use
----------

#. Under **Recruitment** > **Applications** > **All Applications**
#. Open any exist application
#. **SEND JOB OFFER** button will generate and send job offer to the applicant
