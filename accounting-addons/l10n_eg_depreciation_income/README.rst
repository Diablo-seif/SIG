Egyptian Tax Depreciation - Income Tax
============================================

This module is an integration between tax depreciation and
income tax module to auto calculate tax depreciation
in income tax calculation

Usage
=====

* Auto Calculate Tax Depreciation in:
    - Net Profit Calculation
    - Income Tax Calculation
